# Investment Application Data Fields

This document lists all the fields collected from users during the 11-step investment application process in the NOLT Finance platform.

## 1. Plan Selection
- **Selected Plan**: Choice between `NOLT Rise` (Growth) or `NOLT Vault` (Fixed).

## 2. Identity Basics
- **Title**: User's prefix (e.g., Mr, Mrs, Ms, Dr).
- **Full Name**: Legal name of the investor.
- **On Behalf Status**: Boolean flag indicating if filling for someone else.
- **Representative Relation**: Relationship to the applicant (if applicable).
- **PEP Status**: Boolean flag indicating if the user is a Politically Exposed Person (should be either Yes or No).

## 3. Personal Details
- **Gender**: User's gender identity.
- **Date of Birth**: Investor's birth date (YYYY-MM-DD).

## 4. Further Details
- **Mother's Maiden Name**: Used for security verification.
- **Religion**: Demographic information (Optional).
- **Marital Status**: Single, Married, Divorced, or Widowed.

## 5. Contact Information
- **Country Code**: International dialing code (e.g., +234).
- **Mobile Number**: Primary phone contact.
- **Email Address**: Digital contact for notifications and certificates.

## 6. Verification
- **BVN**: 11-digit Bank Verification Number.
- **NIN**: 11-digit National Identity Number.

## 7. Address Details
- **State of Origin**: User's home state.
- **State of Residence**: Current primary location.
- **Home Address**: Full residential street address.

## 8. Next of Kin
- **NOK Full Name**: Legal name of the emergency contact/beneficiary.
- **NOK Relationship**: User's relationship to the contact.
- **NOK Address**: Residential address of the next of kin.

## 9. Secure Vault (Document Uploads)
- **Government ID**: Image/PDF of Passport or National ID card.
- **Proof of Address**: Image/PDF of Utility bill or tenancy agreement.
- **Bank Statement**: Image/PDF at least 3-Month Bank Statements.
- **Selfie-upload (Optional)**: Image upload

## 10. Investment Configuration
- **Investment Amount**: The principal amount to be invested.
- **Target Amount**: Specific financial goal (for Rise plans).
- **Rollover Option**: Instruction for maturity (Principal & Interest, Principal Only, or Payout).
- **Tenure**: Duration of the investment in months (e.g., 6, 12, 18, 24).

## 11. Indemity & Signature
- **Indemity Agreement**: Downloadable indemnity agreement.
- **Signature Canvas**: Sign first and tick acceptance check box before proceeding

## 12. Payment Verification
- **Transfer Receipt**: Proof of manual bank transfer (Image or PDF).
- **Payment Status**: System-tracked state (e.g., PENDING_PAYMENT).
